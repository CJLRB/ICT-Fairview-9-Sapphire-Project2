Tapsilog

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tsp ground white pepper
  • 2 cups cooked garlic fried rice
  • 1 sunny-side-up egg

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, marinate the beef with soy sauce, garlic, sugar, and pepper. Let it sit for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry the marinated beef until tender and browned. Set aside.
  3. Serve the beef with garlic fried rice and sunny-side-up eggs on the side.
  4. Enjoy your delicious Filipino breakfast!
