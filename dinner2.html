Palak Paneer

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 250g fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, minced
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp cream (optional)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash the spinach leaves thoroughly and blanch them in boiling water for 2-3 minutes. Transfer to ice water and then blend into a smooth paste.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add garlic, ginger, and green chili. Saut√© for a minute, then add chopped onions. Cook until golden brown.
  4. Add turmeric powder and tomato puree. Cook until the mixture thickens and oil starts to separate.
  5. Stir in the spinach paste and season with salt and garam masala. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Gently add the paneer cubes and cook for another 3-4 minutes. Stir in cream if using.
  7. Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.
