Chicken Adobo

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine soy sauce and garlic. Marinate the chicken for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pot. Remove chicken from marinade and fry until lightly browned. Set aside.
  3. Pour the marinade into the pot and bring to a boil.
  4. Add water, bay leaves, and ground black pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Return the chicken to the pot. Add vinegar and let it simmer for another 10-15 minutes.
  6. Adjust seasoning to taste. Serve hot with steamed rice.
