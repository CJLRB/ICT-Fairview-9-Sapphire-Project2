Masala Dosa

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dosa batter
  • 2 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and green chilies. Saut√© until onions are translucent.
  3. Add turmeric powder, salt, and mashed potatoes. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes. Set aside.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan and pour a ladle of dosa batter. Spread it into a thin circular layer.
  5. Cook until the edges lift and the bottom is golden brown.
  6. Place the prepared potato mixture in the center of the dosa. Fold it over and serve hot with chutney and sambar.
