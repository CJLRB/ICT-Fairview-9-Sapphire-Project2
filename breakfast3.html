Rice Porridge (Congee)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup jasmine rice
  • 6 cups water or chicken broth
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Optional toppings: boiled eggs, shredded chicken, green onions, soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice thoroughly under cold water.
  2. In a large pot, combine the washed rice, water or chicken broth, and salt.
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium-high heat, then reduce to a simmer.
  4. Cook uncovered for 1 to 1.5 hours, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
  5. Adjust the consistency by adding more water if needed.
  6. Serve hot with your choice of toppings such as boiled eggs, shredded chicken, or soy sauce.
