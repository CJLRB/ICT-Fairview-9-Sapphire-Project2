Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef flank steak, sliced thinly
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1/2 cup water or beef broth
  • 1 tsp sesame oil (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, and cornstarch. Add the beef slices and marinate for 15 minutes.
  2. Blanch the broccoli florets in boiling water for 2 minutes, then transfer to ice water to retain their color.
  3. Heat oil in a wok or skillet. Stir-fry the marinated beef until browned and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  4. In the same wok, saut√© garlic and ginger until fragrant. Add the broccoli and stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  5. Return the beef to the wok and pour in the sauce. Add water or beef broth and stir until the sauce thickens.
  6. Drizzle with sesame oil if desired. Serve hot with steamed rice.
