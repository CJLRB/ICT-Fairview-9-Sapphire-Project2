Kare-Kare

Ingredients

  • 1 kg oxtail (or beef shank), cut into serving pieces
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup annatto seeds (or annatto powder)
  • 1 banana heart, sliced
  • 1 bunch string beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 bunch bok choy
  • 1/2 cup rice flour, toasted
  • 5 cups water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Bagoong (shrimp paste) for serving

Instructions

  1. Boil oxtail (or beef shank) in water until tender. Skim off scum that floats to the surface.
  2. In a small bowl, soak annatto seeds in hot water. Strain and set the colored water aside.
  3. In a pan, heat the peanut butter and diluted rice flour. Stir until smooth and thickened.
  4. Add the cooked oxtail and annatto water to the sauce. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Add banana heart, string beans, and bok choy. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with bagoong on the side.
